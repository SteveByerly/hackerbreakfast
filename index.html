    Welcome to Hacker Breakfast!

    Hacker Breakfast is a weekly meeting of local people interested in all things technology. Bring your laptop and come work on personal projects, bounce ideas off of people, and get to know fellow nerds in the area.

    Schedule

    We meet every Sunday at 10:00am. Most people stick around for a couple of hours, but there is no schedule. Swing by any time!
